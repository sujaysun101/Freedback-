## Competitive Analysis

### Visual Feedback Tools
- **MarkUp.io**: Focuses on visual annotation and collaboration
- **Filestage**: Review and approval workflow
- **GoVisually**: Visual feedback collection

### Project Management Tools
- **Asana**: Task management
- **Trello**: Kanban board management
- **Monday.com**: Project tracking

### AI Writing Tools
- **Jasper**: Content generation
- **Copy.ai**: Marketing copy
- **Grammarly**: Writing assistance

### Our Unique Wedge
**FeedbackFix** uniquely focuses on translating vague, natural language feedback into specific, actionable design tasks - a gap not addressed by existing tools.
